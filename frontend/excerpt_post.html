Please be careful when inputing stuff. Currently there's no way to delete from the client side. I have to manually remove the files. Also, you can put in a link using <a href="https://**link**">link text</a> This isn't necessary; you could paste the plain text, but it won't have the cute blue that you can click. It will just appear as plain text.

In practice, you can write something like this in the content box:
Look at this cool <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gregorian_chant">wikipedia page</a>!

It will appear as:
Look at this cool wikipedia page!

NOTE: To those who know this stuff, I use "text.innerHTML = excerptData.body.replace(/\n/g, '<br>');" to load the excerpts. That means you can do fun html, css, and js stuff if you so please. Your welcome.
